My Journey:

I began my journey to becoming a front-end developer and designer back in 2018. I was encouraged to give coding a try by relatives who are full-time software engineers. I knew I had nothing to lose so I gave it a shot. Little did I know I would fall in love with the process of designing and developing UIs and UXs. But even before coding, I was fascinated by technology. When I was born in 2002 no one was talking about touchscreens, self-driving cars, or the thought of VR and AR experiences. I was given an iPhone 3GS around 2010 and it was such a mesmerizing piece of technology. I still have it, though it sadly no longer operates, and I compare it to today's iPhone and it makes the 3GS look really bad. That phone captivated my attention and I wanted to create the apps I used and the websites I visited. When I was introduced to coding, I was finally able to fulfill that desire, but after I did my first project I couldn't stop. Years later I'm still in love with it all as I constantly aim to go even further on every new project I start.
